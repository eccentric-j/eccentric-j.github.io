About Eccentric J

I've been designing and developing personal and commercial projects for about a decade now. I got started by learning how to hack online computer games by rewriting values to known memory addresses, since then my passion is in web app development. Currently, I live in New York City employed as a lead engineer at VenueBook where I am responsible for software architecture, development, and product design.
